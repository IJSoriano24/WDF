What is the Student Companion Guide App?

The Student Companion Guide App is an all-in-one platform that helps students manage assignments, schedules, campus navigation, events, and essential college contacts. It’s designed specifically for students attending colleges in Ireland.

How do I set my college?

During onboarding, you simply select your college from the list. The app then customises your dashboard, map, contacts, and events to match your campus.

Can teachers upload assignments directly to the app?

Yes. Teachers and administrators can upload assignments, CA briefs, deadlines, and resources. Students can then view all their academic tasks in one organised place.

Does the app help new students navigate campus?

Absolutely. The interactive map helps you find classrooms, lecture halls, offices, and other campus buildings—ideal for students who are new to the college or switching campuses.
